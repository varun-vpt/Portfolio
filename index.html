Directorial
Universe

Patrick Memphis, a name synonymous with groundbreaking
narratives, is renowned for weaving emotions, drama, and
unforgettable characters into a visual tapestry.

With every film, he dives deep, bringing forth the unsaid,
the unseen, and the deeply felt.

BIO

Originating from the lively streets of Melbourne, Australia, Patrick Memphis is
a revered director, celebrated for his distinct cinematic touch. He's showcased 15
features at illustrious film festivals such as Lirewood, Osprey, Fernbrooke, and
SilverQuill.

MY APPROACH

Film, for me, has always been more than just a visual medium; it's a canvas
where human emotions, landscapes, and stories converge. Every frame I craft is a
blend of my experiences, dreams, and the myriad tales I've encountered on my
journey. With each film, I aim to bridge the gap between reality and artistry,
taking audiences on an immersive journey that resonates deep within.

EDUCATION

2004-2008

Elmswood Film Academy, New York

Bachelor of Fine Arts in Film Direction, under the guidance of renowned filmmakers, Patrick honed his directorial vision and craft, culminating in his acclaimed thesis film, "Shadows of Tomorrow."

2000-2002

Greenwood College, CaliforniaAssociate's Degree in Screenwriting

It was here that Patrick's passion for cinema transformed from a hobby into a career aspiration. His first screenplay, "Lost Letters," won the college's prestigious Silver Pen Award.

2002-2003

Lyon Workshop for Visual Storytelling, Paris France

A transformative three-month intensive where Patrick was introduced to avant-garde European cinema techniques, forever shaping his storytelling.

2002-2003

Advanced Workshop in Comedy Direction Humorville Film Institute, Sydney

Focused on modern comedic narratives and the artof comedic timing in storytelling.

BODY OF WORK

Whispers of the City

Exploring the complex terrains of emotion and musical connection, this film won accolades at various international festivals including the Lumière Festival.

Strings of Solitude

Gripping urban drama that captures the metropolitan life and the souls that breathe within its confines. Nominated for 'Best Direction' at the Montage Awards.

The Tides Between Us

An oceanic odyssey that dives deep into human relationships and the secrets they hold. Premiered at the Valencia Film Gala, receiving widespread critical acclaim.
